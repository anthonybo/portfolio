About Me

After attending California State University Chico where I studied computer science, I began working as an Application Support team member for Superlative, a web hosting company. In this position I gained exposure to the development process and the various languages that were used and maintained. This drove my passion to push further into the field, which I matured through an accelerated Web Development Program.

In my free time, I enjoy going to the beach, maintaining a gym routine and tinkering on my gaming servers where I am able to learn new and interesting things that others can enjoy as well. Programming as a career has become a dream and passion of mine that drives me to practice and hone my skills to push myself further into the development community.
